Mentoring Groups

We are excited to be starting a new mentoring groups initiative here at Girl Dev. We realize how important mentoring, continuously learning and building on a skill set is. We also love learning in small groups because it has many advantages. Most importantly, it helps you understand things better because you might find yourself teaching someone else. And we all know, by teaching you discover things you didn't know before and it enhances your learning experience. You also learn to collaborate, explain your findings and motivate one another to go further.

We are seven mentors who are excited to share our knowledge with those who are willing to learn! The groups will have a maximum size of four and the mentor-ship will span over three months. There will be a final deliverable which will be the result of the collaboration between all the different groups.

The topics that we will be mentoring are the following:

  • HTML and CSS
  • JavaScript and JQuery
  • Databases and Big Data
  • Java
  • Ruby
  • The non technical side of software development

To join us you must be self motivated, driven to learn, committed, excited about working with a small group and learning from your peers and mentor. We are also focusing on female developers who are at an intermediate or senior level. Check out Ladies Learning Code for introductory level courses, and hopefully we'll see you here afterwards.

So if this sounds like you please complete this assessment so we can learn more about you and your skill level.

If you have any questions or you are interested in mentoring you can contact me Saba El-Hilo . You can also visit our meetup page for more information about the group.
